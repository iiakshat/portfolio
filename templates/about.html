About Me

Hello!

I'm Akshat Sanghvi — a Machine Learning Engineer who graduated from Parul University in Vadodara, Gujarat. I have a deep passion for Python, Machine Learning, AI, and NLP. My tech journey has been all about getting hands-on: building, breaking, and debugging my way through real-world projects.

I previously interned at Salesken, where I fine-tuned Wav2Vec2 for automatic speech recognition on a Hinglish dataset — combining my love for languages and cutting-edge models. I’m especially fascinated by voice tech, large language models, and the future of multimodal AI.

Outside of tech, I enjoy video editing using Blender and After Effects, and I can spend hours tweaking frames or renders just for that perfect finish. When I’m not coding or editing, I’m likely gaming, binge-watching something nostalgic, or lost in a YouTube rabbit hole of AI, devlogs, or comedy skits.

I’m always open to cool collaborations or just chatting about anything Python, ML, or memes. Feel free to reach out at jakshat569@gmail.com.
